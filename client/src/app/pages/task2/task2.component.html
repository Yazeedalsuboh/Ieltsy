What Is IELTS Writing Task 2?

IELTS Writing Task 2 is an essay-writing task where you are required to respond to a point of view, argument, or problem. Your answer should be at least 250 words and written in a formal style. You must clearly present your opinion, support it with relevant examples, and organize your ideas logically into clear paragraphs. Focus on demonstrating a strong range of vocabulary and grammar, maintaining coherence and cohesion, and answering the question fully and directly. It’s crucial to manage your time well—spend about 40 minutes on this task as it contributes more to your final writing score.
