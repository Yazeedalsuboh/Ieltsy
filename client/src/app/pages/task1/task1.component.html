What Is IELTS Writing Task 1?

IELTS Writing Task 1 requires you to describe, summarize, or explain information presented in a visual format such as a graph, chart, table, map, or process diagram. Your response should be at least 150 words and written in a formal style. Focus on presenting key trends, comparing data, and highlighting significant features without including personal opinions. It’s important to organize ideas clearly, use a range of vocabulary and grammar accurately, and avoid copying directly from the question. Be mindful of time management—spend about 20 minutes on this task to ensure enough time for Task 2.
